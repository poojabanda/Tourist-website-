Famous Places in Jammu & Kashmir

Srinagar

The summer capital of Jammu and Kashmir, Srinagar is famous for its serene Dal Lake, charming houseboats (shikaras), and beautiful Mughal gardens. It's often called the "Venice of the East."

Vaishno Devi

One of the holiest Hindu shrines, dedicated to the Goddess Vaishno Devi. Millions of pilgrims undertake a challenging trek up the Trikuta Mountains to seek blessings at this sacred cave temple.

Watch Video

Amarnath

A major pilgrimage site, Amarnath Cave is famous for the natural formation of an ice Shiva Lingam. It is believed to be the cave where Lord Shiva explained the secret of life and eternity to Parvati.

Watch Video

Gulmarg

Translating to "Meadow of Flowers," Gulmarg is a world-renowned skiing destination. It boasts the highest golf course in the world and a breathtaking gondola ride with panoramic Himalayan views.

Watch Video

Pahalgam

Situated at the confluence of streams flowing from Sheshnag Lake and the Lidder river, Pahalgam is a serene town known for its picturesque beauty and as a base for trekking and the Amarnath Yatra.

Watch Video

Sonamarg

Known as the "Meadow of Gold," Sonamarg is a stunning valley surrounded by majestic glaciers and serene lakes. It serves as a gateway to Ladakh and offers incredible opportunities for trekking and camping.
