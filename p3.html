Famous Places in Himachal Pradesh

Shimla

The capital city of Himachal, Shimla is a classic hill station known for its colonial architecture, bustling Mall Road, and the scenic Ridge. It offers stunning views of the surrounding Himalayan peaks.

Watch Video

Manali

Nestled in the Beas River Valley, Manali is a paradise for adventurers and nature lovers. It's the gateway to Solang Valley for skiing and paragliding, and the starting point for treks to Spiti and Ladakh.

Watch Video

Dharamshala

Home to the Dalai Lama, Dharamshala and its upper town, McLeod Ganj, are a hub of Tibetan culture. Visit ancient monasteries, enjoy serene landscapes, and embark on the famous Triund trek.

Watch Video

Spiti Valley

A cold desert mountain valley, Spiti is a land of stark beauty and ancient monasteries. It's perfect for adventurous travelers seeking remote landscapes, rugged roads, and a unique cultural experience.

Watch Video

Kasol

Situated on the banks of the Parvati River, Kasol is a haven for backpackers and trekkers. Known as 'Mini Israel,' it offers stunning valley views and is the starting point for the Kheerganga trek.
